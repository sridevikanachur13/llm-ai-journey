*   Curiosity is the willingness to question beyond the known, driving both individual and societal progress by exploring what interests us, rather than settling for immediate answers.
*   It is inherently generative, fostering resilience by reframing failures as investigations and promoting deeper learning through better questions and tolerating ambiguity.
*   Curiosity enriches everyday life, strengthens relationships through genuine interest, and compounds over time to build a broad, interconnected understanding of the world.
